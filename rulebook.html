PRISM GAMES

EIGHTS & STAKES

Official Rulebook

01

GAME OBJECTIVE

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards by matching suits or values while using action cards to outplay opponents.

02

SETUP

  • Standard 52-card deck with 2 jokers
  • 2 to 6 players
  • Every player starts with 7 cards
  • Remaining cards become the draw pile
03

ACTION CARDS

2 +2 cards
8 Wild card
JOKER +4 stack
A Reverse
Q♠ Skip
10 Burn
04

STACKING RULES

Draw cards can stack.

  • 2 can stack on 2
  • Joker can stack on Joker or 2
  • If a player cannot respond, they draw the full stack
05

LAST CARD RULE

When a player reaches one remaining card, they must announce “LAST CARD”.

06

THE LOOP

If the draw pile empties, the discard pile reshuffles while keeping the current top card active.
